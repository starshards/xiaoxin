# Personal IP

## Identity

- Petite adult woman with long black or dark-cobalt wavy hair, a soft center-parted curtain fringe, large dark eyes, and a calm small smile.
- Black cord with a small warm-gold rectangular pendant.
- Permanent vivid red bow on her anatomical left side of the hair (viewer right in a front view).
- Permanent pink square smartwatch on her anatomical left wrist (viewer right in a front view).

## Character modes

- **Main IP:** 2.25–2.75 heads tall, petite capable adult proportions. Summer uniform: white black-trim sleeveless tank, high-waisted black or deep-cobalt sickle-cut pants, and small low-profile off-white mesh athletic sneakers. Cover the waist and ankles. Shoes stay close to natural foot length, with a thin sole and no chunky silhouette. Use `../assets/star-main-ip-reference.png`.
- **Chibi sticker:** 1.8–2.0 heads tall with a huge round head, cloud-like cobalt hair, tiny short torso, short rounded limbs, and simplified hands. Summer uniform: white dark-blue-trim sleeveless tank, bright cobalt loose full-length pants compressed onto the short chibi legs, and tiny simple off-white sneakers. The rounded trouser legs extend from waistband to shoe openings with no exposed calves, socks, or ankles. Use it for expressive page-corner accents and small sticker scenes, not as a replacement for the main IP in substantial compositions. Use `../assets/star-chibi-ip-reference.png`.

## Optional visual grammars

Choose the grammar that best explains the current content. Do not use more than one as the main metaphor.

- **End-to-end journey:** a small bridge, stepping stones, or an open door = a path that has become available.
- **Untangling complexity:** a knot, thread ball, drawer, or funnel changes from tangled to ordered.
- **Judgment and trade-off:** two small routes, a turning sign, or a balanced scale makes a decision visible.
- **Evidence and clarity:** a lantern, lens, or single gold mark reveals a reliable answer from uncertainty.

## Never draw

- Xiaohei, a black creature, a default engineer mascot, a tall fashion figure, a large office blazer, bare midriff, oversized shoes, a missing bow, or a missing smartwatch.
