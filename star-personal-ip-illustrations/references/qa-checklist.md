# QA Checklist

Pass every item before delivery:

- 16:9, pure white background, generous empty space.
- The same petite adult Q-version woman is recognizable: long black curtain-fringe hair, dark eyes, black cord, small gold pendant.
- The summer outfit is intact: white black-trim tank, high-waisted black sickle-cut pants, off-white mesh sneakers; no exposed waist or ankles.
- The character performs the key action.
- The chosen visual grammar clarifies the idea rather than forming a dense diagram. Cards, gold line, sparkle, and door are optional, not required.
- Flat clean cartoon lines; not realistic, painterly, corporate, or childlike.
- No title, long labels, logo, code, laptop, or accidental extra objects.

Regenerate when the character becomes tall, glamorous, a generic anime woman, a child, an office worker, a different person, when the shoes become oversized, or when it mechanically repeats the card-and-gold-line motif.
