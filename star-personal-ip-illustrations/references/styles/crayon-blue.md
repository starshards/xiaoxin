# Crayon Blue

Use this profile for personal, editorial, friendly, and playful illustrations.

- Pure white background with generous negative space. Keep it completely free of decorative scribbles unless the user explicitly asks for them.
- Render the character with tactile wax-crayon or oil-pastel grain, visibly uneven hand-drawn strokes, and bold cobalt-blue outlines.
- Use dense ink-dark cobalt scribbles for hair and trousers; keep skin and the tank mostly off-white.
- Use soft pale-pink cheek blush. Keep warm gold only for the small rectangular pendant or one meaningful accent.
- Keep the silhouette readable without a sticker halo on a white background.
- Draw small off-white athletic sneakers with a visibly breathable woven-mesh upper, compact rounded toe, and thin flexible sole. Never use chunky or oversized footwear.
- Preserve the same petite adult 2.25–2.75-head identity; the handmade medium must not turn her into a child mascot.
