Generate one standalone 16:9 horizontal Chinese article illustration. Use `assets/star-summer-ip-reference.png` as the identity reference. Select one style through `style-dna.md` and use its matching reference image.

```text

Selected style: {clean-line or crayon-blue}. Follow only the matching profile in `references/styles/` and do not mix the two rendering systems.

Recurring character: a petite adult woman, 2.25–2.75 heads tall; long black hair with soft center-parted curtain fringe; large clear dark eyes; a calm, intelligent expression; black cord necklace with a small warm-gold rectangular pendant. Summer outfit: normal-length white sleeveless tank with thin black trim, high-waisted black sickle-cut pants with roomy curved legs and tapered cuffs, small low-profile off-white breathable mesh athletic sneakers. Shoe length stays close to natural foot length, with a compact rounded toe and thin sole; never chunky or oversized. The high waist meets the tank hem: no exposed midriff or ankles. She is small but capable, never a child, baby mascot, tall fashion model, or generic office woman.

Core idea: {one idea}
Action: {what she is physically doing}
Visual metaphor: {one physical metaphor}

Choose exactly one visual grammar that matches the idea:
- connection and delivery: blank cards → thin gold line → gold sparkle;
- end-to-end journey: a small bridge, stepping stones, or a door;
- untangling complexity: a knot, thread ball, drawer, or funnel becoming ordered;
- judgment and trade-off: two small routes, a turning sign, or a balanced scale;
- evidence and clarity: a small lantern, lens, or gold mark revealing a reliable answer.

Do not add cards, a gold line, a sparkle, or a door when the chosen grammar does not need it.

No text, title, logo, laptop, code, charts, office scenery, realistic portrait, or unrelated props unless the requested scene specifically requires one. Do not make a formal flowchart. Keep the character responsible for the central action.
```

For edits, state the exact element to change and explicitly say every other identity, selected-style, and composition detail must remain unchanged.
