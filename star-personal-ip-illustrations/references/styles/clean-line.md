# Clean Line

Use this profile for formal review decks, explanatory articles, workflows, and delivery narratives.

- Pure white background with generous negative space.
- Flat Q-version cartoon forms with clear, elegant black outlines.
- Narrow palette: black hair and main-IP trousers, white tank, deep navy only when needed, off-white sneakers, vivid red bow, pink smartwatch, and warm gold for the pendant or one meaningful accent.
- Restrained flat fills; no painterly grain, crayon scribbles, gradients, or glossy shading.
- Keep objects simplified and readable rather than realistic.
- Preserve a calm, capable adult presence without making the character tall, glamorous, corporate, or childlike.
