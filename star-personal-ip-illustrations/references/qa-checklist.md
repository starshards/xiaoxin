# QA Checklist

Pass every item before delivery:

- 16:9, pure white background, generous empty space.
- The same woman is recognizable: long wavy curtain-fringe hair, dark eyes, red bow on her left side, pink square smartwatch on her left wrist, black cord, and small gold pendant.
- The selected character mode is intact: `main-ip` uses 2.25–2.75 heads, high-waisted sickle-cut pants, and small mesh sneakers; `chibi-sticker` uses 1.8–2.0 heads, bright cobalt loose full-length pants compressed onto short legs, tiny limbs, and tiny off-white sneakers. Neither mode exposes calves or ankles.
- Exactly one style profile is active. The output does not accidentally mix clean black vector-like linework with cobalt crayon rendering.
- For `clean-line`: black outlines, restrained flat fills, and no crayon grain.
- For `crayon-blue`: cobalt wax-crayon texture, clean empty white background, pale-pink cheek blush, and small low-profile woven-mesh sneakers.
- The character performs the key action.
- The chosen visual grammar clarifies the idea rather than forming a dense diagram. A door or other metaphorical prop appears only when it supports the idea.
- Rendering matches the selected style; never realistic, corporate, or childlike.
- No title, long labels, logo, code, laptop, or accidental extra objects.

Regenerate when the character becomes tall, glamorous, a generic anime woman, an unrelated child mascot, an office worker, or a different person; when the bow or watch disappears or swaps sides; when the shoes become oversized; when the two character modes are accidentally blended; or when it mechanically repeats an old composition.
