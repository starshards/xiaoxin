# Personal IP

## Identity

- Petite adult woman in Q-version proportions: 2.25–2.75 heads tall.
- Long black hair with a soft center-parted curtain fringe, large dark eyes, calm small smile.
- Black cord with a small warm-gold rectangular pendant.
- Summer uniform: white black-trim sleeveless tank, high-waisted black sickle-cut pants, small low-profile off-white mesh athletic sneakers. Cover the waist and ankles. Shoes stay close to natural foot length, with a thin sole and no chunky silhouette.

## Optional visual grammars

Choose the grammar that best explains the current content. Do not use more than one as the main metaphor.

- **Connection and delivery:** blank cards = scattered requirements; thin gold line = independent connection and implementation; gold sparkle = a verified result.
- **End-to-end journey:** a small bridge, stepping stones, or an open door = a path that has become available.
- **Untangling complexity:** a knot, thread ball, drawer, or funnel changes from tangled to ordered.
- **Judgment and trade-off:** two small routes, a turning sign, or a balanced scale makes a decision visible.
- **Evidence and clarity:** a lantern, lens, or single gold mark reveals a reliable answer from uncertainty.

Use the card-and-gold-line grammar only when the illustration is about connecting scattered pieces into a working result.

## Never draw

- Xiaohei, a black creature, a default engineer mascot, a tall fashion figure, a large office blazer, bare midriff, cropped pants, or oversized shoes.
